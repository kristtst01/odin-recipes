Pulled Pork Recipe

Description

Pulled pork is a succulent, melt-in-your-mouth dish made by slow-cooking pork until it's tender enough to shred easily. This version is done in a slow cooker or oven and finished with a flavorful BBQ sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pork Shoulder (about 1.5kg)
  • Salt 1 tbsp
  • Black Pepper 1 tbsp
  • Smoked Paprika 1 tbsp
  • Brown Sugar 2 tbsp
  • Garlic Powder 1 tsp
  • Onion Powder 1 tsp
  • BBQ Sauce 1 cup
  • Optional: Apple Cider Vinegar 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix the salt, pepper, smoked paprika, brown sugar, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork shoulder.
  3. Place the pork in a slow cooker or a Dutch oven.
  4. If using a slow cooker, cook on low for 8 hours. If using an oven, cover and cook at 150Â°C for 4-5 hours.
  5. Once cooked, remove the pork and shred it using two forks.
  6. Mix in the BBQ sauce and apple cider vinegar (if using) until well coated.
  7. Serve in buns, tacos, or with a side of coleslaw.
