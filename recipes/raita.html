Raita Recipe

Description

Raita is a cooling yogurt-based condiment packed with refreshing flavors of cucumber and spices. It's creamy, lightly tangy, and perfect for balancing out spicy dishes or as a fresh dip on its own.

Ingredients

  • Plain Yogurt 250g
  • Cucumber (grated or finely chopped) 1 small
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin Powder 1/2 tsp
  • Fresh Cilantro (chopped) 2 tbsp
  • Mint Leaves (optional, chopped) 1 tbsp
  • Black Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place the yogurt in a bowl and whisk until smooth.
  2. Add the grated or chopped cucumber and mix well.
  3. Stir in the salt, cumin powder, and black pepper.
  4. Fold in the chopped cilantro and mint leaves (if using).
  5. Chill in the fridge for at least 10 minutes before serving.
  6. Enjoy as a refreshing side with naan, rice, or grilled meats.
